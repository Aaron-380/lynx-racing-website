Who Are We?

A diverse collective mapping the future of electric motorsport.

Our Core Mission

Lynx Racing is built on a foundation of diverse talent unifying behind a single, unwavering vision: absolute performance.

We are a highly functional network of structural engineers figuring out the minutiae of load paths, aerodynamicists sculpting airflow around high-speed bodies, electrical engineers writing the firmware that drives raw power, and business analysts ensuring the team maintains operational pace.

Through relentless practical application of advanced theory, we aim not only to compete but to dominate collegiate electric motorsport competitions on a global scale.
