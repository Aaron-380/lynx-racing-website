About Lynx

Pushing the boundaries of automotive engineering and electric performance.

Our Story

Born out of a relentless passion for speed and engineering excellence, Lynx Racing was founded to challenge the status quo of competitive electric racing. As a premier racing team, we pour countless hours into designing, fabricating, and optimizing state-of-the-art vehicles from the ground up.

We are a dedicated collective of structural engineers, aerodynamicists, electrical specialists, and business minds working as one cohesive unit. Our mission goes beyond just crossing the finish line first—it's about developing the next generation of top-tier engineering talent equipped to tackle the world's most complex challenges.

The Evolution of Performance

The transition to electric powertrains demands a complete paradigm shift. With our latest technological platforms, we are redefining what is physically possible on the track. From cutting-edge carbon-fiber composite layups to highly advanced custom battery management systems, every single component is scrutinized and pushed to its absolute kinetic limit.

Our commitment to continuous innovation means every iteration of our vehicles is lighter, faster, and more aerodynamically efficient than the last. We are not just building cars; we are engineering pure, unadulterated victory.
