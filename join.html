Why Join Lynx Racing?

We are a student-led organization at UNSW focused on pushing the boundaries of automotive engineering and electric performance. By joining the team, you will get hands-on experience designing, fabricating, and assembling a highly sophisticated race car from the ground up.

We're looking for passionate individuals specializing in:

  • Mechanical Engineering (Chassis, Suspension, Aerodynamics)
  • Electrical Engineering (High Voltage Systems, Telemetry, Software)
  • Business & Operations (Sponsorship, Media, Finance)
